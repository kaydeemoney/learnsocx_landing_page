Facebook Marketing FAQ

What is Facebook Marketing and why is it important?

Facebook Marketing refers to using Facebook’s platform to promote products, services, or brands. With over 2.8 billion monthly active users, it offers a massive audience for businesses to increase their visibility.

What will I learn in this Facebook Marketing course?

This 3-month course covers optimizing Facebook advertisements with AI, running search, conversion, and display ads, and using Facebook Marketplace for product sales.

How is this course structured to help me succeed?

It’s self-paced, allowing students to learn at their own speed while completing assignments before deadlines.

Are there partnership opportunities after this course?

Yes, after two months, you can partner with LearnSocx to apply your skills and start earning with real-world projects.

What are the requirements to join this course?

Basic computer knowledge, internet, and electricity are all you need to start. By the end, you’ll be equipped with the skills to run successful ad campaigns.
